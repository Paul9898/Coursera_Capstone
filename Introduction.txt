The data set we select is about collisions in Seattle. Usually, some severe traffic accidents may cause big traffic jams. It is neccessary to take timely actions to prevent or reduce traffic congestions. But how? From the brief report from the privies, the weather report, the conditions of the position, and so on, the information may help instructors make predictions. If there may happen a severe collsion, traffic station could cut down the car flow and prevent more cars from driving in the accident position. Therefore, a prediction model is needed to evaluate the severity of specific collisions.
